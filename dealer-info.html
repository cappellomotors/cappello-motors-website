Michigan Updates for Car Dealers

State of Michigan Updates

  • Feb 20, 2025: SOS reminds dealers—titles must be filed within 15 days of sale (MCL 257.217).
  • Jan 1, 2025: New electronic title rules—dealers must use e-Services for lien releases.
  • Dec 31, 2023: Surety bond increased to $25,000 for all active licenses—check yours!

Compliance Alerts

Stay sharp—here’s what Michigan expects:

  • Keep a Police Book—record all sales per MCL 257.251.
  • Class B dealers: Complete training every renewal (SOS Dealer Manual).
  • No Sunday sales in counties over 130,000—know your spot!
